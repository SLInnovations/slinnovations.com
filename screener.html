Know where to begin before you teach a single lesson

Starting writing instruction without a clear picture of where a student is means guessing. Too far back and you lose them to boredom. Too far forward and you lose them to frustration.

The Writing Skills Placement Screener gives you a concrete starting point. It assesses the foundational writing skills that matter most for explicit, systematic instruction, scores automatically, and tells you clearly where to begin.

The screener works whether or not you're using Structured Steps to Writing. The Scope & Sequence and Student Tracker included in the bundle are useful alongside any explicit writing program, or on their own to track progress over time.
