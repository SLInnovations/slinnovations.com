FOUNDATIONS IS JUST THE BEGINNING!

Framework and Composition tiers are currently in development. Students who complete Foundations move directly into paragraph writing, essay planning, and multi-genre composition.

Four tools. Used well. From the first sentence to the final essay.

The same 4 tools show up across all 12 units. Students learn them once. Then they grow with them.

SPARK + SORT

Solve the blank page problem

Before a student writes a single word, they use SPARK to pour out every idea freely, then SORT to choose their strongest ideas and land on a clear focus. The blank page stops being scary. Interactive app included in Step Forward.

STEMS

Build every paragraph

Every sentence in a paragraph has a name and a job. Students learn STEMS once and use it across every unit, every genre, every level. The framework never changes. The writing grows.

CAMPS

Catch every error

A systematic editing process students internalise rather than guess at. Capitalisation, Agreement, Meaning, Punctuation, Spelling. In order, every time.

CHECKLISTS

Open and close with confidence

Introduction and conclusion checklists make the hardest paragraphs learnable, repeatable, and teachable. Students check every element before moving on.
