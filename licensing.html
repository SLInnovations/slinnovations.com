Common questions

How does licensing work?

Licences cover a set number of educators at a single site. Each licensed educator gets their own access to the program files and tools. Licences are not transferable between sites. All licensing is invoiced directly — contact us and we'll handle the quote and payment process.

Can we pay by purchase order?

Yes. School, learning centre, and district licences are invoiced directly and can be paid by purchase order, cheque, or bank transfer. Get in touch and we'll send you a formal quote.

What if we need more educators added later?

You can upgrade your licence at any time. You pay the difference between your current licence level and the next tier — not the full price of a new licence. Contact us and we'll work it out.

Do licences include professional development?

Not currently, though we are developing professional learning options. If your school or district is interested in PD alongside a licensing purchase, mention it in your inquiry and we can discuss what's possible.

When will the full program be available?

Foundations (Units 1–4) is available now. Framework and Composition tiers are in development. If you're purchasing a licence now, you'll receive Foundations immediately and all future units as they are released at no additional cost.

We're a small tutoring practice. Which option is right for us?

If you have 2–5 tutors, the Small Team licence is your best option. If you're a sole practitioner, the individual licence covers everything you need. Not sure? Reach out and we'll help you figure out the right fit.
